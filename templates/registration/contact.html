Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

Browse available rooms, select your dates, and complete the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation once the booking is created.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can view and manage your bookings from the "My Bookings" section in your account.

How do I become a hotel owner?

Sign up for an account and select the "Hotel Owner" role during registration. You can then list and manage your hotels.
